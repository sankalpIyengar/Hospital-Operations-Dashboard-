# Hospital Operations Dashboard

## Overview

This project is an Excel-based interactive dashboard designed to analyze hospital performance across revenue, patient volume, and operational efficiency.

## Objective

To provide actionable insights for hospital management by analyzing:

* Patient flow
* Revenue distribution
* Length of stay (LOS)
* Case mix and payer contribution

## Tools Used

* Microsoft Excel / WPS Office
* Pivot Tables
* Charts & Visualizations
* Slicers for interactivity

## Key Metrics

* Total Revenue
* Total Cases
* Average Length of Stay (LOS)
* Case Mix Index (CMI)
* Revenue per Case
* Revenue per Day (Derived Metric)

## Key Insights

* Inpatient cases generate the majority of revenue
* Insurance contributes the largest share of revenue
* Higher LOS in IP cases indicates complex treatments or inefficiencies
* Revenue growth is primarily volume-driven

---

## Business Impact

This dashboard helps stakeholders:

* Identify high-revenue services
* Optimize resource allocation
* Monitor operational efficiency
* Understand payer dependency
  
## Dataset

The dataset is a synthetic hospital dataset containing:

* Patient details
* Doctor information
* Case types (IP, OP, DC)
* Revenue and LOS metrics
* Insurance/payer information
